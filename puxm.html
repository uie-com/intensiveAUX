Persuasive UX Metrics

Show the value of UX through our free 5-day Intensive — March 29 - April 2 — led by Jared Spool.

Over 5 days, you’ll identify and implement your team’s UX metrics, using a proven framework that starts with defining your organization’s UX Outcomes. You’ll then identify the critical measurements your organization will need to deliver well-design products and services. You’ll package all this in an action plan you can roll out right away.

  • Are you struggling to focus your organization on your user’s needs?

  • Do you need to elevate the value of your UX efforts to your executives, stakeholders, and other teams?

  • Is everyone clear on how you’ll deliver well-designed user experiences?

  • In this 5-day intensive, Jared will show you how persuasive UX metrics will drive an improved user experience throughout your organization.
